About Temp E-mail

Temp E-mail is a free, secure, and anonymous temporary email service designed to protect your privacy from spam, unwanted subscriptions, and malicious attacks. With our service, you can get an instant disposable email address without any registration. Use it to sign up for websites, download content, or test applications without exposing your real email.

Our service is fast, reliable, and completely free. We do not store any of your personal information, and all emails are automatically deleted after a short period, ensuring your anonymity. Whether you're a developer, a privacy-conscious user, or just need a quick email for a one-time task, Temp E-mail is the perfect solution.

Why Use a Temporary Email?

In today's digital world, your email address is a valuable piece of personal information. It's often required for everything from signing up for social media to making online purchases. However, sharing your main email address with every service can lead to a flood of spam, marketing emails, and a higher risk of data breaches. A temporary email address acts as a shield, giving you the ability to interact with online services without compromising your primary inbox.

  • Ultimate Privacy: Keep your personal identity and email address hidden from third parties.
  • Spam-Free Inbox: Say goodbye to junk mail and promotional newsletters you never wanted. Our disposable emails handle all the clutter.
  • Enhanced Security: Prevent phishing attacks and malware from reaching your main email account by using a temporary address for suspicious websites.
  • Testing and Development: Ideal for developers and QA testers who need to test email functionality for their applications without creating new accounts every time.
  • Instant Activation: Get a new email address with just a single click. No forms, no personal details, and no waiting.
  • Customizable: Choose your own username and select from a list of available domains to create a professional-looking temporary email.

How to Use Temp E-mail

  1. Generate an Email: Click "Get Random Email" to instantly create a new address or use the form to create a custom one.
  2. Copy and Use: Copy the new email address to your clipboard and use it wherever you need.
  3. Receive Emails: Your inbox will automatically refresh, and any emails sent to your temporary address will appear here in real-time.
  4. Read and Delete: View the full email content and delete it when you're done. Your temporary email and its contents will be permanently removed after a certain period.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Temp E-mail?

Temp E-mail is a free service that provides you with a temporary, anonymous, and disposable email address. You can use it to receive emails for a limited time and then discard it.

How does it work?

We use the mail.tm API to generate a temporary email address for you. You can use this address to receive emails, which will appear in your inbox. No registration or personal details are needed.

Yes, our service is 100% free. We are supported by ads to keep the service running and accessible to everyone.

Your emails are secure and private. They are automatically deleted after a short period, and we do not store any logs or personal data.
